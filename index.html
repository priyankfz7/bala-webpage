Who is Gagan Jaggi sir ?

Gagan (CA - ATC), mentor by profession, has 15 years of experience of teaching and guiding students towards the path of success. Gagan founded Bala Institute in 2008 with a vision of applying his ideas and teaching philosophies with no boundaries. He has put his heart and soul to Bala Institute to make it successfully running centre in Noida. With lots of love and success recieved from our students and alumni, we are in process to launch a new branch of Bala Institute in Green Park. Bala Institute is not just about the classroom study, but we also concentrate on regular worksheets and assessments to keep a tab on each student's growth and focus on their weak points wherever and whenever needed. We are always eager to have backup classes for students to let each student cover his syllabus head to toe. With an aim to keep 8 students per batch, we are more than successful in providing personal attention to give proper guidance and conceptual clarity to each of our students. Bala institute has a mission to hand-hold their students through out their course. That's why Bala Institute was running smoothly during Covid affected times when Gagan sir took an initiative to shift the mode of teaching to video conferences. You must visit Bala Institute once to realise it's true potential and feel the difference.
